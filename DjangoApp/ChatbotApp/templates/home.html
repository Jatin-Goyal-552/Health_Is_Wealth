Our Services

We provide services such as Covid Chatbot, Corona Virus Prediction by X-ray and Disease Prediction by Symptoms etc. I hope I will grow it more.

Covid Chatbot

It is a chabot from which you can ask anything about COVID-19. It will answer your all queries about Covid.

Disease Prediction By Symptoms

A model that can predict diseases by knowing symptoms from you. It will also tell you description, precautions and symptoms of predicted disease.

Corona Virus Prediction

This model will predict whether a chest X-ray have corona virus or not. You just have to upload the image of chest X-ray.
